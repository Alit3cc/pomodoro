What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a proven time management method by Francesco Cirillo: work for 25 focused minutes, then take a 5-minute break. After four sessions, reward yourself with a longer 15–30 minute break to fully recharge.

Structured intervals prevent mental fatigue, sharpen concentration, and make large tasks feel approachable. This timer works completely offline — no account, no ads, no tracking.
